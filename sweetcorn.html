Sweetcorn

You can use tinned sweetcorn or fresh for most things. Obviously tinned is easier - just drain it and mix through the rest of your bowl.

If you use fresh corn on the cob, you can buy it still all wrapped up in its green coat or at the supermarket it's usually already undressed ðŸ˜³

Then you can brush it with some oil, salt and pepper, maybe some chilli and cook it on a griddle or frying pan until you see brown cooked patches all over the corn. You'll have to turn it a few times whilst it's cooking. Then when it's cool enough to handle, get a knife and cut off all the kernels.
