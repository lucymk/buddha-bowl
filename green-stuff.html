Spinach

You can get different types of spinach - the one you get at the supermarket in bags is usually baby spinach. It's delicate and you don't need to cook it. You can use it raw, or stir it through hot stuff just before you eat and it will go a bit softer.

If you buy a bunch of spinach from the greengrocer, it is more likely to be mature spinach! It's a bit tougher and earthier. It can still be eaten raw, but you'll probably want to shred it. Otherwise you can stir it through hot food as with baby spinach, or saut√© it with some lemon and garlic.
